At Tabibi Law Firm, we help injured people take back control of their lives after serious accidents. We are a Los Angeles personal injury law firm serving clients across California who are dealing with pain, stress, lost income, and medical bills after a car accident or other serious injury.

We do not represent insurance companies or corporations. We represent real people who are hurt, overwhelmed, and looking for someone they can trust. Whether your case involves a car crash, motorcycle accident, rideshare collision, or a slip and fall, our team is here to guide you through it and protect your rights every step of the way.

If you have been injured, you may not know what to do next. You might be without health insurance, unsure how to get treatment, and already feeling pressure from the insurance company. This is where we step in. We connect you with medical providers so you can start treatment immediately without paying anything out of pocket. We take over all communication with the insurance companies so you can focus on healing. And we build a strong case to help you recover the maximum compensation for your injuries, time off work, and everything you have been through.

We treat every client like family. That means we listen, we check in, and we always keep you informed. At our firm, communication is a priority. You will never feel ignored or left in the dark. We take pride in being responsive, honest, and easy to work with. From the very first call, we want you to feel cared for and confident that your case is in the right hands. When you call Tabibi Law Firm, you speak directly with an experienced attorney who will explain everything clearly and help you understand your options. There are no consultation fees. You do not pay anything upfront. And we only get paid when we win your case.

If you were injured in Los Angeles or anywhere in California and need help with a personal injury claim, we are here to support you from start to finish and fight for the outcome you deserve.
